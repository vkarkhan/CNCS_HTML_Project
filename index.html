Who we are

We are a team of computational scientists having doctoral and masters degrees in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Computer Science. Our varied engineering backgrounds result in unique combination of pool of experts in scientific data analysis in the fields such as fluid dynamics, computational physics, finance, communication engineering, advanced embedded systems, Very-Large-Scale-Integrated (VLSI) circuits, and antenna & microwave technology. We acquired and honed these skills over the period of several years, both in the capacity of- graduate researchers, and industrial experts. We are passionate about developing efficient computational solutions for several real-world engineering problems.
